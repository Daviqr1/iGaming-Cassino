Stacked Wild Symbol
Stacked Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatter symbol.
Bikini Ladies Multiplier
During any spin, when 1 or more Stacked Wild symbols appear fully on any reels, all wins for that spin will be multiplied by those Stacked Wild symbols' corresponding multipliers.
Stacked Wild symbol appearing fully on reel 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 awards x1, x2, x3, x4 and x5 multiplier respectively.
When Stacked Wild symbols occupy all of the reels, 5000x of the total bet amount is awarded.
Free Spins Feature
3, 4 or 5 Scatter symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the Free Spins Feature with 8, 12 or 20 free spins respectively.
When 1 or more Stacked Wild symbols appear partially on any reels during any free spins, the Stacked Wild symbol(s) will be nudged into its reels fully.
Free spins can be retriggered.
