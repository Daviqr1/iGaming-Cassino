Treasures of Aztec
Treasures of Aztec is a 6-reel, 5-row video slot (with an additional reel on top of reel 2, 3, 4 and 5) featuring Wilds-on-the-Way and increasing multipliers in both main game and free spins.
The game is played with 20 base bet for 2,025 ways - 32,400 ways, bet level of 1 to 10 and bet size of 0.05 to 10.00.
The in-game currency is $.
The Bet Size is set using the "Bet Size" selector.
The Bet Level is set using the "Bet Level" selector.
The Bet Amount is set using the "Bet Amount" selector.
"Cash Wallet" displays the cash available for wager.
"Auto Spin" automatically plays the game for a selected number of rounds.
Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the "Paytable".
A bet way win in cash is equal to the value shown in the "Paytable" multiplied by the bet size and bet level.
Bet ways win if the winning symbols are in succession from the leftmost reel to the right.
Total number of winning bet ways for each symbol are calculated by multiplying the number of adjacent winning symbols on each symbols from the leftmost reel to the right.
The winning symbol payout is multiplied by the number of winning bet ways.
Simultaneous wins on different bet ways are added.
After the payout of every round is made, all winning symbols will explode allowing the symbols above them to cascade down for a new round.
Additional winning combination will be tallied in every round until no more winning combination can be tallied.
All wins shown in cash.
Some symbols in reels 2, 3, 4 and/or 5 may occupy 2 to 4 symbols' spaces and each of these symbols will represent as a single symbol during payout calculations.
Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatter symbol.
Wild symbol appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only.
Free Spins Feature is played at the same bet size and bet level as the spin that triggered the Free Spins Feature.
Wilds-on-the-Way
During any spins, some symbols (excluding Wild symbol and Scatter symbol) in reels 2, 3, 4 and/or 5 that occupy 2 to 4 symbols' spaces may have a silver frame around itself.
At every new round after the new symbols have cascaded down, any silver-framed symbol(s) that is involved in a win in the previous round will be transformed into a randomly selected symbol (other than Wild symbol and Scatter symbol) with a gold frame around itself.
At every new round after the new symbols have cascaded down, any gold-framed symbol(s) that is involved in a win in the previous round will be transformed into Wild symbol.
Multiplier
At the start of any main game spin, the win multiplier is x1.
During any spins, if there are one or more winning symbols on the reels, after wins are paid and the new symbols have cascaded down, the win multiplier will be increased by 1.
Free Spins Feature
4 Scatter symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the Free Spins Feature with 10 free spins. Each additional Scatter symbol will trigger 2 more free spins.
At the start of the Free Spins Feature, the win multiplier is x2.
During any free spin, if there are one or more winning symbols on the reels, after wins are paid and the new symbols have cascaded down, the win multiplier will be increased by 2.
Free spins can be retriggered.
