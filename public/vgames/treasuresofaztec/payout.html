Some symbols in reels 2, 3, 4 and/or 5 may occupy 2 to 4 symbols' spaces and each of these symbols will represent as a single symbol during payout calculations.
Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatter symbol.
Wild symbol appears on reels 2, 3, 4 and 5 only.
Wilds-on-the-Way
During any spins, some symbols (excluding Wild symbol and Scatter symbol) in reels 2, 3, 4 and/or 5 that occupy 2 to 4 symbols' spaces may have a silver frame around itself.
At every new round after the new symbols have cascaded down, any silver-framed symbol(s) that is involved in a win in the previous round will be transformed into a randomly selected symbol (other than Wild symbol and Scatter symbol) with a gold frame around itself.
At every new round after the new symbols have cascaded down, any gold-framed symbol(s) that is involved in a win in the previous round will be transformed into Wild symbol.
Multiplier
At the start of any main game spin, the win multiplier is x1.
During any spins, if there are one or more winning symbols on the reels, after wins are paid and the new symbols have cascaded down, the win multiplier will be increased by 1.
Free Spins Feature
4 Scatter symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the Free Spins Feature with 10 free spins. Each additional Scatter symbol will trigger 2 more free spins.
At the start of the Free Spins Feature, the win multiplier is x2.
During any free spin, if there are one or more winning symbols on the reels, after wins are paid and the new symbols have cascaded down, the win multiplier will be increased by 2.
Free spins can be retriggered.
Feature Buy
Tap on Feature Buy button to open the Feature Buy menu.
Tap on Start button to buy the Free Spins Feature at the price displayed in the Feature Buy menu.
