Songkran Party
Bikini Paradise is a 5-reel, 4-row video slot featuring stacked Wild symbols with multipliers.
The game is played with 25 bet lines (fixed), bet level of 1 to 10 and bet size of 0.04 to 8.00.
The in-game currency is à¸¿.
The Bet Size is set using the "Bet Size" selector.
The Bet Level is set using the "Bet Level" selector.
The Bet Amount is set using the "Bet Amount" selector.
"Cash Wallet" displays the cash available for wager.
"Auto Spin" automatically plays the game for a selected number of rounds.
Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the "Paytable".
A bet line win in cash is equal to the value shown in the "Paytable" multiplied by the bet size and bet level.
Only the highest win per bet line is paid.
Bet lines win if the winning symbols are in succession from the leftmost reel to the right.
Simultaneous wins on different bet lines are added.
All wins shown in cash.
Stacked Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Scatter symbol.
Free Spins Feature is played at the same bet size and bet level as the spin that triggered the Free Spins Feature.
Bikini Ladies Multiplier
During any spin, when 1 or more Stacked Wild symbols appear fully on any reels, all wins for that spin will be multiplied by those Stacked Wild symbols' corresponding multipliers.
Stacked Wild symbol appearing fully on reel 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5 awards x1, x2, x3, x4 and x5 multiplier respectively.
When Stacked Wild symbols occupy all of the reels, 5000x of the total bet amount is awarded.
Free Spins Feature
3, 4 or 5 Scatter symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the Free Spins Feature with 8, 12 or 20 free spins respectively.
When 1 or more Stacked Wild symbols appear partially on any reels during any free spins, the Stacked Wild symbol(s) will be nudged into its reels fully.
Free spins can be retriggered.
