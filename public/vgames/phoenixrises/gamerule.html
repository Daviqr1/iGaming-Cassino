Phoenix Rises
Phoenix Rises is a 5-reel, 3-row video slot featuring Free Spins Feature with increasing multipliers up to x150.
The game is played with 20 base bet for 243 ways (fixed), bet level of 1 to 10 and bet size of 0,02K to 1,25K.
The in-game currency is Rp.
The Bet Size is set using the "Bet Size" selector.
The Bet Level is set using the "Bet Level" selector.
The Bet Amount is set using the "Bet Amount" selector.
"Cash Wallet" displays the cash available for wager.
"Auto Spin" automatically plays the game for a selected number of rounds.
Winning combinations and payouts are made according to the "Paytable".
A bet way win in cash is equal to the value shown in the "Paytable" multiplied by the bet size and bet level.
Bet ways win if the winning symbols are in succession from the leftmost reel to the right.
Total number of winning bet ways for each symbol are calculated by multiplying the number of adjacent winning symbols on each symbols from the leftmost reel to the right.
The winning symbol payout is multiplied by the number of winning bet ways.
Simultaneous wins on different bet ways are added.
All wins shown in cash.
Wild symbol substitutes for all symbols except Free Spins symbol.
Wild symbol appears on reels 2, 3 and 4 only.
Free Spins Feature is played at the same bet size and bet level as the spin that triggered the Free Spins Feature.
Free Spins Feature
5, 6 or 7 Free Spins symbols appearing anywhere will trigger the Free Spins Feature with 12 free spins.
If the triggering game has 5 Free Spins symbols, the win multiplier in the free spins then starts at x1 and every free spin increases the multiplier by 1.
If the triggering game has 6 Free Spins symbols, the win multiplier in the free spins then starts at x2 and every free spin increases the multiplier by 2.
If the triggering game has 7 Free Spins symbols, the win multiplier in the free spins then starts at x3 and every free spin increases the multiplier by 3.
The maximum multiplier value achievable is x150.
5 Free Spins symbols appearing anywhere during the Free Spins Feature will award 12 more free spins.

Additional Information
The game will automatically return to its initial state after being idle for 90 days. Progress and status of any collection games, unfinished free games, and unfinished feature games etc will no longer be retained.
Malfunction voids all pays and plays.
In the event of disputes over game results, all game results that are shown in the game history page are absolute and final.
